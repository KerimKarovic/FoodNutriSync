Upload a complete BLS dataset file (TXT or CSV format)

Technical Information

  • Endpoint: PUT /admin/bls-dataset
  • Supported Formats: TXT (tab-separated), CSV (comma-separated)

  • Required Columns: SBLS (BLS Number), ST (German Name)

  • Authentication: Admin role required
